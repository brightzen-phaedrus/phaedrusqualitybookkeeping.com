## ✅ Connected to Phaedrus Quality Bookkeeping

Your QuickBooks Online account has been successfully connected. PQB now has access to perform automated bookkeeping quality checks on your account.

### What happens next?

- PQB will begin analyzing your chart of accounts and transaction history
- Quality checks run automatically as part of BrightZen's Weekly Accounting service
- You'll receive reports highlighting categorization issues, anomalies, and reconciliation gaps

### Need help?

Contact us at [phaedrus@brightzen.com](mailto:phaedrus@brightzen.com).

You can disconnect PQB at any time from your [QuickBooks Online settings](https://app.qbo.intuit.com).
