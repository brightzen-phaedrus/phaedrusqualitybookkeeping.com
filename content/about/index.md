## What is Phaedrus Quality Bookkeeping?

Phaedrus Quality Bookkeeping (PQB) is an internal integration built by [BrightZen, Inc.](https://brightzen.com) that connects directly to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API.

It automates the routine bookkeeping tasks that eat up hours every week — categorizing transactions, reconciling accounts, pulling reports, and flagging anomalies — so the humans can focus on the decisions that actually matter.

## Who built this?

PQB was built by the team at BrightZen as part of their [Weekly Accounting](https://weeklyaccounting.com) service. It's not a standalone product — it's a tool that makes Weekly Accounting's bookkeeping service faster, smarter, and more consistent.

## How does it work?

PQB connects to a QuickBooks Online company using OAuth 2.0 (the standard Intuit authorization flow). It reads financial data, performs automated quality checks, and surfaces insights through BrightZen's internal systems.

**PQB does not:**
- Store your QuickBooks credentials
- Share your data with third parties
- Make unauthorized changes to your books

All access is governed by the permissions you grant during the OAuth connection process, and you can disconnect at any time from within QuickBooks.
