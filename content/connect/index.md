## Connect Your QuickBooks Online Account

Phaedrus Quality Bookkeeping connects to your QuickBooks Online account to perform automated bookkeeping quality checks as part of BrightZen's Weekly Accounting service.

### Before you connect

- You must have admin access to the QuickBooks Online company you're connecting
- Review our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) and [Terms of Service](/terms/)
- PQB will request read and write access to your accounting and payment data

### How it works

1. Click the connect button below to authorize with Intuit
2. Sign in to your QuickBooks Online account
3. Review and approve the permissions
4. You'll be redirected back here once connected

### Ready?

Contact [phaedrus@brightzen.com](mailto:phaedrus@brightzen.com) to initiate the connection process. We'll walk you through authorization and verify everything is set up correctly.
